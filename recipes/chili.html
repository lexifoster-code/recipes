Chili CornBread Bowl

Is it cold out? are you hungry? I have just the thing for you !

A delicious and healthy meal full of protien and veggies sure to satisfy even the meat-eaters in your family.

Ingrediants

  • vegetable broth
  • sweet onions
  • 1 small red pepper
  • 1 small yellow pepper
  • gabonza beans(chikpeas)
  • white beans
  • diced tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • chili seasoning
  • meat subtitute of choose

Steps

  1. prepare meat substitute to your liking
  2. set meat substitute to the side
  3. dice all of your vegetables
  4. add oil to sautee pan
  5. put diced veggies in the sautee pan(exclude tomatoes)
  6. cook veggies until translucent
  7. In one pot add cooked veggies and meat sub..
  8. add diced tomatoes and its juices
  9. turn stove on to meadium heat
  10. add vegetable broth
  11. add all seasoning to your liking id suggest tasting your meal step by step
  12. add all of your beans. *juices not inlcuded*
  13. halfway through cooking add in tomato paste
  14. stir and turn heat down and let simmer for 30 minutes
  15. Wanting to add something more to fill out your meal? try a vegan cornbread. trust me you wont be dissappointed.
  16. and lastly dont forget the GARNISH!!
