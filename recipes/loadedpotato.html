super loaded potato

Is it cold out? are you hungry? I have just the thing for you !

A delicious and healthy meal full of protien and veggies sure to satisfy even the meat-eaters in your family.

Ingrediants

  • russet potato
  • green onions
  • sour cream
  • velvetta shredded cheese
  • diced meat *can be anything you want*
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • butter

Steps

  1. cook meat of your choose
  2. dice your meat into chunks
  3. wash your potatoes
  4. poke holes into the potato with a fork on all sides
  5. put potatoes in a pan or place directly on oven racks
  6. cook potaotes until fully tender
  7. split potoato down the middle
  8. scoop insides of the potato into a bowl
  9. add sour cream and seasoning to the bowl
  10. add a splash or two of milk
  11. mix contents of the bowl until smooth
  12. add potaoto filling back to the potatoe skins
  13. top the potato with your meat chunks
  14. sprinkle cheese to your liking over potaotes
  15. place potoatoes back into the oven to melt the cheese
  16. slice green onions
  17. last but not least garnish with green onions and parsley
  18. pssstttt.... hey.. you still there ? Ok Great! drizzle with bbq sauce and ranch for some extra fineese.
