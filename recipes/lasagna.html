Lasagna

Aye yo you! you hungry ? well lets make a meal

A gooey decadent lasagna that is sure to please anyone from the cheese lover to the picky eater.

Ingrediants

  • ricotta
  • sweet onions
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 medium egg
  • lasgana noodles
  • 3 pounds of lean ground beeg *ground turkey for a even leaner option
  • tomato paste
  • 2 cans of hunts four cheese pasta sauce
  • colby jack cheese
  • mozerella cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • italian seasoning
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and season genoursly
  2. Boil lasagna noodles till aldente
  3. set noodles aside and let them cool
  4. dice all of your vegetables
  5. add oil to sautee pan
  6. put diced veggies in the sautee pan
  7. cook veggies until translucent
  8. In one pot add cooked veggies and browned ground beef
  9. add tomato paste , pasta sauce , and italian seasoning
  10. turn stove on to meadium heat
  11. stir ocassionally
  12. turn pot down to low and let meat sauce simmer
  13. grab ricotta and mix with egg *dont make this mixture thick*
  14. spread ricoota mixture to the bottom of an aluminum pan
  15. layer noodles on top
  16. add more ricotta mixture to the edges of lasagana noodles
  17. layer on meat sauce
  18. layer on colby jack and mozerella cheese
  19. layer on genrerous amount of pasta sauce
  20. repeat steps 14-19 or until lasagna reaches desired thickeness
  21. lastly once lasagna is layered throughly add plenty of cheese on top
  22. put in the oven for 1 hour and 30 minutes
  23. pair with garlic bread and cole slaw for a creamy savory dish.
